We are Deadlands


We are an up-and-coming metalcore band based in Long Island, New York. Our focus is to break the stereotype of heavy music and show that there is something for everyone to enjoy.

We never want to fit into one box. What Deadlands dreams of is to evolve with the audience that supports them and grows with them. While we always want to stem from distorted voices and intensity, we write what comes to us and what feels natural. The eclectic sound is what finds our music suitable for everyone.


With the rise of our music, we’re currently expanding our collection of gear and skills every single day to make every single show a unique experience. Check out our 'Tour' page for when we’ll be playing near you.
